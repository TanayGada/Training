## What is LLD?
- we transform high-level architectural concepts into detailed component-level designs
- It involves 
    1. breaking down a software system into smaller, manageable components and 
    2. specifying how they will interact to achieve the desired functionality
- It ensures that the software is 
    1. well-structured, 
    2. modular, and 
    3. easy to maintain.



## Why LLD is important?
1. Ensures Detailed Implmentations of each component
2. Early Detection of Flaws in design phase only
3. Effective Collaboration to create coherent and consistent design
4. Easier to maintain and scale


## HLD vs LLD
1. HLD
    - provides high-level view of the system's structure
2. LLD
    - component interactions, 
    - data flow, and 
    - specific algorithms

- LLD complements HLD by translating abstract ideas into practical implementation details.


## How to tackle LLD Problems 
1. Clarify requirements and constraints 
2. Break down problem into mangageable components, defining their responsibilities
3. Select appropriate design patterns to address specific design challenges.
4. Ensure your design meets the given requirements and constraints.
5. Clearly explain your design choices and justify them
